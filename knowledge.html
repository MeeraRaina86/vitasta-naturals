Knowledge Hub

Discover the benefits and uses of our Kashmiri treasures.

Pure Kashmiri Saffron

Health Benefits: A powerful antioxidant, Saffron is known to improve mood and memory (a 'sunshine spice'). It's also prized for its anti-inflammatory properties and can promote a glowing complexion.

Simple Recipe (Kesar Doodh): Gently warm a cup of milk. Add 3-4 strands of Vitasta Saffron and a pinch of cardamom. Stir and let it steep for 5-10 minutes. Sweeten with honey or jaggery. Enjoy this nourishing drink before bed.

Premium Mamra Almonds

Health Benefits: Mamra almonds are a nutritional powerhouse, richer in healthy oils and Vitamin E than other varieties. They are excellent for brain health, help manage cholesterol, and are great for healthy skin and hair.

Simple Recipe (Healthy Snack): Soak a handful of Mamra almonds overnight. In the morning, peel the skin and enjoy them raw for a powerful start to your day. You can also blend them with dates and oats to make healthy energy bites.

Finest Kagzi Walnuts

Health Benefits: Walnuts are one of the best plant-based sources of Omega-3 fatty acids, which are crucial for heart and brain function. Their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory compounds also support gut health and can help manage type 2 diabetes.

Simple Recipe (Yogurt Topper): Coarsely chop a few Kagzi walnuts and lightly toast them in a dry pan for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Drizzle them over a bowl of yogurt with a spoonful of honey for a perfect, crunchy breakfast.
